1983 Honda CM 250 Custom

History:

Browsing on Facebook MarketPlace, I stumbled upon this classic. I happened to find it the same weekend as the KLR, but decided to chase down both anyway!
A very nice lady was selling it after someone had attempted to steal the bike a week before! They cut the ignition wires and dented the gas tank, but they failed to take the bike!
I knew that this project wouldnt need to much to get going, for $600 I practically felt like I was stealing it. WE GOT IT RUNNING THAT NIGHT!

Stats!

  • Starting Mileage: 28,900
  • Drum Brakes: Front & Rear! (Hope they don't kill me!)
  • Reupholstered leather seat: Comfy
  • ToolKit: Honda original
  • 6V Electrical: Dim and sad
